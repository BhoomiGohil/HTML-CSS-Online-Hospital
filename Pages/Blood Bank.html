Blood Bank

The Blood Bank at Saifee Hospital is a modern up-to-date facility offering advanced Blood Component Therapy. Being the one The member of Federation of Bombay Blood Banks, Blood bank provides 24x7 services and 100% voluntary Blood collection with state-of the art testing facilities that follows proper systems, to ensure good quality of blood. The Blood Bank is open round the clock and throughout the year. Voluntary donors are welcome between 9.00 am to 5.00 pm.
